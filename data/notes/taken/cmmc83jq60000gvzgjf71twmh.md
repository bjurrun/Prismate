This document outlines the visual identity and brand principles for Project Alpha. Following the GRIP method, we ensure that every design element serves a specific intent and contributes to the overall clarity of the platform.

Key pillars for our visual language:

* Quietness: Minimize distractions through generous whitespace...

* Focus: Use high-contrast elements only for primary actions.
